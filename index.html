Who We Are

About Us

Established in 2014 at NMAMIT, the Robotics Club is an interdisciplinary club providing practical, hands-on experience in robotics. As the official robotics club of the institute, it exposes students across disciplines to complex projects — from designing and building mechanical and electronic bots to conducting workshops on IoT, 3D modelling, and programming, while competing at national and international events.

What We Do

Our Divisions

Combat Robotics

Race bots, Soccer bots, Sumo bots and War bots — we focus on design, testing, fabrication, and control at a competitive level.

Soft Robotics

Line followers, maze solvers, and IoT hobby projects — exploring the gentler, smarter side of autonomous machines.

Aero Division

Drones and rockets designed, fabricated, and launched — the sky is literally not the limit.
